Defend

2D Unity 5 game

Welcome to Defend

Play the web version: Here
Download the Windows and Android version: https://github.com/Teh-Lemon/Defend-Game/releases

Note for Chrome users: Google recently disabled NPAPI support by default, which the Unity Web Player relies on. To play the web version of Defend, you can either:

  • Download the standalone or Android version of the game
  • Use an alternative browser such as Firefox
  • Re-enable NPAPI support here chrome://flags/#enable-npapi
    • Will not work after Sept 2015

How to play

  • Defend your base from the waves of meteors
  • Your ammo slowly refills over time
  • Use the mouse to aim and shoot
  • Power ups:
    • Shield - Re-activates your shield
    • Turret - Activates, up to 2, friendly AI turrets to assist you
    • Big Bullet - Fire bigger heavier bullets for 5 seconds
    • Refill Ammo - Refills your ammo to max capacity
